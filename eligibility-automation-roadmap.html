The Problem We're Solving

Your team manually checks patient insurance eligibility before appointments — logging into portals, calling carriers, verifying coverage details. This takes hours every day, and when the information is wrong, it creates downstream problems: surprise patient bills, denied claims, and rework.

This project automates that process. The system will pull your upcoming appointments from Eaglesoft, automatically verify each patient's eligibility through your existing insurance connections, and surface the results for your team — flagging anything that needs human attention. Your staff stops doing data entry and starts reviewing data.

Discovery & Observation

Weeks 1–2

Before building anything, I need to understand exactly how eligibility works in your office today — not how it's supposed to work, but how it actually works. I'll sit with your insurance coordinator, watch the process end-to-end, and document every step.

What I'll be mapping
  • The full workflow from appointment booking to eligibility confirmation
  • Which insurance carriers make up the majority of your patients
  • Where time is spent and where errors typically occur
  • How Eaglesoft and Vyne Trellis are currently used in the process
  • Baseline measurements: time per day on eligibility, current accuracy
What I'll need from your team
  • Access to shadow the person who handles eligibility for a half day
  • Eaglesoft version number and server access details
  • Current Vyne Trellis tier and feature information
  • A signed Business Associate Agreement — download template here
You'll receive
  • A written map of your current eligibility workflow
  • Time audit showing where hours are spent
  • A clear technical plan for the automation build

Technical Setup & Validation

Weeks 2–4

I'll connect to your Eaglesoft system and eligibility data source to confirm everything works before building the full solution. This is the "measure twice, cut once" phase — making sure the data flows correctly and the integration is solid.

What's happening
  • Setting up a secure connection to your Eaglesoft server
  • Testing that I can pull appointment schedules and patient insurance data
  • Connecting to the eligibility verification API and validating response quality
  • Setting up HIPAA-compliant cloud infrastructure with proper encryption
  • Identifying any technical constraints and resolving them early
What I'll need from your team
  • Admin access or coordination with your IT provider for Eaglesoft server access
  • Remote access setup (VPN or similar) so I can work without being onsite daily
You'll receive
  • Confirmation that the integration works with your specific setup
  • Architecture overview showing how data flows securely between systems
  • Monthly infrastructure cost estimate
3

Build the Core System

Weeks 4–8

This is where the automation gets built. The first version focuses on doing one thing well: checking tomorrow's patients automatically and giving your team a clear view of who's verified and who needs attention.

What the system will do
  • Pull the next day's appointment list from Eaglesoft automatically each evening
  • Check insurance eligibility for every scheduled patient
  • Parse the response: active/inactive, plan type, deductibles, maximums, co-pay percentages
  • Flag incomplete or problematic responses for manual follow-up
  • Display results on a simple dashboard — green (verified), yellow (review needed), red (problem)
What it won't do yet
  • Real-time checks at the moment of booking (that comes later)
  • Submitting claims or modifying any data in Eaglesoft
  • Replacing human judgment — staff always has final say
You'll receive
  • A working system that checks eligibility for tomorrow's patients
  • A staff-facing dashboard showing results at a glance
4

Parallel Testing

Weeks 8–10

This is the phase that addresses the accuracy question directly. For two weeks, the automated system runs alongside your existing manual process. Staff keeps checking eligibility as they normally would, and we compare the automated results against their findings every day.

How it works
  • The system checks eligibility automatically — staff checks manually as usual
  • Results are compared daily to identify any discrepancies
  • Every mismatch gets investigated: was the system wrong, or was the manual check wrong?
  • Edge cases are fixed as they surface

This is zero risk to the practice. Nothing changes about your current process — we're just running the new system in parallel to prove it works. If the accuracy isn't there, we fix it before anyone relies on it.

You'll receive
  • An accuracy report showing match rate over the testing period
  • Documentation of any edge cases found and how they were resolved
  • A clear recommendation on whether the system is ready for daily use
5

Go Live

Weeks 10–14

Once the parallel test proves the system is accurate, your team transitions to using it as the primary eligibility check. Staff stops manually verifying patients the system marks as confirmed, and only reviews the ones that are flagged.

What changes
  • Green patients: verified automatically, no manual check needed
  • Yellow patients: system found something worth reviewing — staff checks these
  • Red patients: clear problem (lapsed coverage, missing info) — staff takes action
  • Adding real-time checks when new appointments are booked
You'll receive
  • Fully operational automated eligibility system
  • Before/after metrics: time saved per day and accuracy rate
  • Staff training on using the dashboard

What Comes Next

Month 4 and beyond

Once eligibility is running smoothly, the same infrastructure opens the door to additional automation — each one following the same approach of observe, build, test, adopt.

Potential next projects
  • Claim scrubbing — catching common errors before claims are submitted
  • Denial tracking — logging patterns to reduce future denials
  • Pre-authorization document assembly — pulling treatment plans, X-rays, and drafting narratives
  • Claim status monitoring — automated follow-up on unpaid claims

We'll prioritize based on what's creating the most pain after eligibility is handled.
